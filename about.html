About ARKISMUN

ARKISMUN

The Abdulrahman Kanoo International School Model United Nations (ARKISMUN) conference is renowned as the largest student-led event in the Kingdom of Bahrain. It is an annual conference that brings students from all over the island together to debate through the understanding and education of international relations and diplomacy.
