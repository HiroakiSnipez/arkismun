ARKISMUN XIII

Led by Secretary General, Jana Abu Sarhan & Director, Eyad Zouinekh

About the Conference

The 13th ARKISMUN conference took place on February 23rd & 24th, 2024, at the prestigious Gulf Hotel Convention Center

The Utopian Mean

ARKISMUN XIII was centered around the concept of "The Utopian Mean" - a state of perfect balance between the good and evil of this world. Throughout the conference, participants indulged in discussions that aimed to find the ideal middle ground that lied amongst the contradictions of mankind. The pendulum swung until together, we balanced the scales.

ARKISMUN XII

Led by Secretary General, Ali Alnahash & Director, Shaikha Albuflasa

About the Conference

The 12th ARKISMUN conference took place on February 24th & 25th, 2023, at the biggest exhibition center in the Middle East - Exhibition World Bahrain.

The Path of Light

ARKISMUN XII revolved around the theme "Path of Light", in which Delegates kindled the light that illuminates humanity’s existence and created a path out of it. We acknowledged that in our world we can either fall into despair and darkness, or we could follow the radiating light, the choice was in our hands. At ARKISMUN XII, we walked down the Path of Light.

ARKISMUN XI

Led by Secretary General, Maria Alawadhi & Director, Maysam Bukamal

About the Conference

The 11th ARKISMUN conference took place on February 25th & 26th, 2022, at the reputable Gulf Hotel Convention Center

The Last Hour

The theme of the ARKISMUN XI conference was ‘The Last Hour’. One that emphasizes the urgency of these global affairs under the ticking of time. It was time to take action before it's too late, before we reach a point of no retreat. Time was in the grasp of our palms, in ARKISMUN XI, we faced The Last Hour.

KMUN X

Led by Secretary General, AbdulRahman AbdulMalik & Director, Noor Almoayad

About the Conference

The 10th ARKISMUN conference took place on April 9th & 10th, 2021, online.

Repairing the Roots

Regardless of how tall and lush we grow, our roots remain tainted. KMUN X found humanity laying six feet under, buried under the roots of its own failures. It was time to visit and strike the core of our history and what established it all, to create a safe environment where we mend the past before its too late. In KMUN X, we Repaired the Roots.
